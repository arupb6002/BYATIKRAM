Planting Hope, Nurturing Tomorrow:
"Byatikram’s Green Mission with NDRF"

On the occasion of World Environment Day, Byatikram Foundation proudly joined hands with the courageous NDRF team in a powerful initiative to nurture nature and secure a sustainable future. With the theme, “If there are trees, there is life,” the event highlighted the vital role of greenery in our survival. Each sapling planted is not just a plant—it is a promise, a symbol of hope for a cleaner, greener, and healthier tomorrow. The presence of NDRF ASI Maneshwar Deuri as the Chief Guest added great significance to the occasion, inspiring everyone with his commitment to both human safety and environmental protection. At Byatikram, we believe that planting trees is only the beginning; the true mission lies in protecting and preserving them. Because when we save the environment, we ultimately save ourselves. Let this be a call to action for all—let’s not just celebrate World Environment Day, let’s live it every day through conscious care for our planet.
