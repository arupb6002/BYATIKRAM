About Byatikram

Byatikram Foundation is a non-governmental organization (NGO) that is committed to catalyzing social change through creative thinking and participative action. Guided by progressive values, the foundation operates in fields ranging from education to culture, creative development to community welfare.

Dedicated to questioning traditional boundaries, Byatikram dreams of a society where everyone is empowered, every voice heard, and every initiative results in long-term impact.
